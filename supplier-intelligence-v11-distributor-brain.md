## EXECUTIVE SUMMARY: THE DISTRIBUTOR'S BRAIN

This document serves **all B2B packaging distributors** (Stretch Wrap, Tape, Polybags, Strapping, Film, Accessories) whose core pain points are:

1. **Minimum Order Value (MOV) constraints** (Freight economics)
2. **Sample velocity bottlenecks** (How fast can you send test products?)
3. **Customer concentration risk** (Too much revenue from too few whales)
4. **Lead quality filtering** (Not all leads close at the same rate)
5. **Decision maker identification** (Ops managers ‚â† CEOs)

**The Goal:** Total operational awareness of the supplier through **5 critical questions**, then infer buyer personas without expensive data APIs.

**The Principle:** Ask only what changes our buyer-finding strategy. Everything else is noise.

# üè† ROOF 1: BUYER'S BUYERS INTELLIGENCE (Adapted for Distributors)

## "Who is this supplier currently serving?"

### **LAYER 1.1: Customer Verification & Industry Mapping**

**Data Nodes:**
- **Customer Industry Clustering** (From website, LinkedIn, testimonials)
  - *Metric:* What industries do you serve? (Food? Logistics? E-commerce? Retail?)
  - *Signal:* **Industry Dominance Score** (e.g., "70% Food Distribution, 20% E-commerce Fulfillment, 10% Retail")

- **Vertical Relevance to Our Buyers**
  - *Example (Tom):* If he serves food distribution heavily, we focus on food logistics companies
  - *Example (Tape):* If they serve small manufacturers heavily, we focus on manufacturing purchasing coordinators

- **Customer Size Clustering**
  - *Metric:* Do you mostly work with small ($500K revenue), mid ($10M), or large ($100M+) companies?
  - *Signal:* Determines what buyer sizes we target

- **Review Sentiment & Retention Signals**
  - *Source:* Google Reviews, Trustpilot, industry forums
  - *Metric:* "Fast shipping," "Reliable quality," "Great service" vs. "Slow delivery," "Quality issues," "Price is high"
  - *Action:* If reviews highlight "Fast shipping," we find buyers who value speed. If "Reliability," we find buyers who value consistency.

**Roof 1 Scoring:**
- If you serve 10+ small-to-mid manufacturing/logistics companies ‚Üí **High Score**
- If you serve mostly Fortune 500 or government contracts ‚Üí **Relevance Risk** (Different buyer profile than SMBs)

---

### **LAYER 1.2: Shipment Continuity & Buying Patterns**

**Data Nodes:**
- **Recurring vs. One-Off Orders**
  - *Signal:* Do customers reorder from you regularly, or are they one-time buys?
  - *Metric:* **Customer Lifetime Value (CLV) potential**
  - *Action:* If recurring, we find long-term buyers. If one-off, we find project-based or seasonal buyers.

- **Order Size Consistency**
  - *Signal:* Do customers tend to order the same size repeatedly, or varied?
  - *Metric:* **Order Predictability**
  - *Action:* Predictable orders = corporate/contract buyers. Varied = tactical/emergency buyers.

**Roof 3 Scoring:**
